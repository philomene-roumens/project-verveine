OUR COMMITMENT

Our aim is to create a whole new way of consuming. The textile industry is one of the biggest pollution contributor in the world, we want to break that, and make fashion sustainable. Our mission is focusing on 3 values : respect of the environment, transparency, and sociability. We work with companies and suppliers that we have selected with our vison, which we have physically visited, and share our values.

OUR PRODUCTS

Our products are all made in Europe, with sustainable materials, coming from fair and organic organizations. Our product are made in France, Italy, Portugal and only travel by train. We avoid the use of harmful chemicals and prefer organic resource : cotton, dye. We want to reduce to the maximum our carbon footprint, to do so, we work with environmental expert, and we have sustainability certification. We want to keep transparency and social values at the heart of our company, we work with suppliers that provide high salaries and good working conditions. We are also developing new ways of producing, by using new materials (recycling) and working with new companies. To go further, all our clothing labels are biodegradable, with Verveine seeds to plant inside: bury the label, drop water, get a nice Verveine plant!

OUR CONTRIBUTION

We also think that we have a role to play at our scale, that is the reason why Once a month, the 7th of each month we donate 10% of our income from our sales to help various charity association. We want to be an inspiration for the fashion community, brands and fashion addict!

SUSTAINABLE SHIPPING

Delivery takes also part in environemental issues, this is why our shipping are mainly made by train. It may take an extra 3 to 4 days for delivery, but if that's the price to save the planet, then let's adopt a sustainable lifestyle

RETURNS POLICY

To avoid unessecary transport and clothes destruction we do not allow return with online shopping. We exceptionally allow returns if the product is considered faulty or taken back to a pop up store.

OUR PARTNERSHIPS AND LABELS

We work with sustainable and ethic partners. All our products are certified with Oeko-tex 100, Fair trade and MADE IN GREEN.
